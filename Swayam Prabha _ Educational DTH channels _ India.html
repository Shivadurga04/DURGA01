Education through DTH

  • DTH (Direct to Home) Satellite TV is the best medium to reach out to the wider population of India. Hon'ble Prime Minister envisaged this and suggested to use DTH medium for wider dissemination of education.

  • Inspired by this vision of Hon'ble Prime Minister and with an objective to reach the unreached segment, particularly in rural, tribal and remote location, Ministry of Education has expanded its DTH presence through a bouquet of 280 channels under PM e-Vidya

  • These educational channels help students/learners from a wide variety of subjects/streams, and are available 24x7 with a structured schedule.
